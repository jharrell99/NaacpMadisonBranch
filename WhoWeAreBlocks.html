Mission - Vision - Core Values

NAACP mission statement is “to secure the political, educational, social, and economic equality of rights in order to eliminate race-based discrimination and ensure the health and well-being of all persons.” The emphasis of this statement is the need to strengthen all institutions in such a way that they contribute to the creation of an ideal American society. 

NAACP vision statement is “to ensure a society in which all individuals have equal rights without discrimination based on race.” The statement represents the leadership vow of this organization and its determination to emerge victoriously. 

NAACP core values include “equality, inclusiveness, respect for all, and integrity.”  To ensure it succeeds in this role, NAACP is organized to have a strong foundation where its stakeholders are seen as role models. Our core values are crucial as they create an institutional culture that promotes the rights of everyone and considers all people equal. NAACP values and strives to spread similar our perspectives across the U.S.
